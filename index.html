ABOUT US

Remine India Private Limited is a Private incorporated on 06 December 2022. It is classified as Non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Uttarakhand.

Its authorized share capital is Rs. 1,000,000 and its paid up capital is Rs. 1,000,000.

It is inolved in Recycling of metal waste and scrap [from rejected aluminum, utensil, containers and other used metallic items etc. Collection of metal waste and scrap for and other used metallic items etc. Collection of metal waste and scrap for recycling is included in 51498.]
